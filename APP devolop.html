Digital Payment Training

Learn Safe and Secure UPI Transactions

Step 1: Install UPI App

Download PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm or BHIM from Play Store.

Step 2: Link Bank Account

Select your bank and verify your registered mobile number.

Step 3: Create UPI PIN

Create a secure PIN and never share it with anyone.

Step 4: Scan QR Code

Use the Scan & Pay option to scan merchant QR codes.

Step 5: Complete Transaction

Verify details and confirm payment using your UPI PIN.

Safety Tips

  • Never share OTP
  • Never share UPI PIN
  • Check recipient details
  • Avoid unknown payment links

Cyber Security Safety Guide

Follow These Tips to Stay Safe Online

1. Never Share OTP

One Time Passwords (OTPs) are for your security. Never share them with anyone, not even bank representatives.

2. Use Strong Passwords

Use uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols. Avoid names and common words.

3. Avoid Unknown Links

Do not click suspicious links from unknown sources. They may steal your personal information.

4. Keep Applications Updated

Update your apps regularly to protect against the latest security threats.

5. Verify Websites Before Login

Check for HTTPS and the padlock icon before entering your username or password.

Stay Alert, Stay Safe!

Being careful online protects you and your loved ones from fraud and cybercrime.
