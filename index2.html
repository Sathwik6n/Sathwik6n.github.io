Digital Literacy Assistant

Improving Digital Literacy and Online Service Usage

About the Project

This project aims to improve digital literacy among community members by providing awareness and training on smartphones, online services, digital payments, and cyber security.

Objectives

  • Assess digital literacy levels
  • Promote online services
  • Provide smartphone training
  • Encourage digital payments
  • Create cyber security awareness

Digital Payment Guide

  1. Install a UPI application
  2. Link your bank account
  3. Create a secure UPI PIN
  4. Scan QR codes for payments
  5. Never share OTPs

Cyber Security Tips

  • Use strong passwords
  • Do not share OTPs
  • Avoid suspicious links
  • Keep apps updated
  • Use trusted websites only
